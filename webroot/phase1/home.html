about me

I am a Computer Science student interested in UI/UX design, human-computer interaction, and the interplay between AI and marginalized communities. The bigger picture, whether it be politically, economically, or environmentally, is something that I’m always considering because it’s an area that genuinely excites me. The constant evolution of technology as well as the historical - fundamental - problems that follow along with that evolution always motivates me to continually improve my skills and my understanding of the solutions to many of our current real-world problems – whether it be solving software problems or better serving a userbase. I aspire to help as many people as I can and allow technological evolution to equally benefit everyone – closing the gaps that technology has opened even wider.
